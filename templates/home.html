Welcome To Our Company

Here, we specialize in manufacturing premium quality WPC (Wood Polymer Composite) doors and frames under our brand RAGwud. Our products are designed to combine the natural look of wood with the strength and durability of modern technology. They are 100% waterproof, termite-proof, borer-proof, and fire-retardant, making them a long-lasting and low-maintenance choice for homes, offices, and commercial spaces. With stylish designs, superior finish, and eco-friendly composition, our WPC doors and frames are the perfect blend of beauty and strength.
